About


This project is open source, you can check it out on Github, you will also find a CLI version on the Github repo if you want to host it locally, and have control over other variables (changing the price fetching mode, and the frequency of refreshing the price).


The website allows you to receive real-time notifications by email for Bitcoin price updates, once the price meets a certain limit (upper&lower limits are allowed), an Email will be sent, and you will be unsubscribed from the service.

FAQ


How often is the price refreshed?

Currently, it's refreshed every 40 seconds. If you want to control this rate, you can try out the local (CLI) version avaliable on Github


What happens if I re-enter a used email address?

Entering an email address that is already subscribed will update the limits associated with the email to the new ones


I recieved an email once the limit was met, why am I not receiving anymore updates ?

Once an email is sent, the email becomes unsubscribed. This happens to prevent redundant emails (a bunch of emails all stating that the price has gone below the limit). However you can re-subscribe to the service instantly.
